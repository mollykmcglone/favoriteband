Sarah and Molly's Favorite Bands!

Sarah is an avid music fan. As a child she played the alto saxaphone and the violin.   Currently she enjoys listening to the Avett Brothers, Brandi Carlise, and Vampire Weekend.

Molly is a less-avid music fan. As a child she played the piano, violin, oboe, trumpet, and guitar. Currently she enjoys listening to almost any music while outside. One of her favorite banks is The National.
